Events App

Date

Nov 04, 2023

Services

UI/UX Design

The Problem

Most events app right now are not user friendly and have a lot of features that are not needed. Most events are found out by word of mouth rather than through the app search. And right now most events app, are just used to find out the location of the event and the time. What if we could make an app that is more user friendly and has more features

How did we know this was a problem?

User Interviews


Conducted a series of user interviews with individuals spanning different age groups to gain perspectives on their experiences with online apps. This allowed me to delve into the pain points they face and develop a deep sense of empathy for their unique needs and challenges.


Gathered valuable information about what truly matters to users when it comes to neighborhood connectivity. These conversations provided a wealth of knowledge, shedding light on their frustrations and desires.



Understanding User Painpoints


More screens to show the complete process of booking and showing available times etc Suggestions have unique ids to users as a field to search instead of just initials. Filter in the search bar to filter based on localities and interests in activities.
